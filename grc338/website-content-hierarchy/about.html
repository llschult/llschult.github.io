About

Sunny Side SLO is a Breakfast Cafe located right on the beautiful Avila beach. The cafe was founded in 1982 by a set of twins who gratuated from Cal Poly, Sunny and Bunny Smith. We hope you enjoy our unique selection of fresh breakfast foods, bakery items, and drinks that are perfect to enjoy by the beach.
