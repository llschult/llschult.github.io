Our Story

Sunny Side is a Breakfast Cafe located in Avila Beach, CA. Founded in 2000 by two Cal Poly grads who wanted a fun breakfast spot by the beach, Sunny Side has been providing guests with delicious food and amazing service ever since. Our unique selection of breakfast foods, fresh bakery items, and thirst-quenching drinks will make your breakfast by the beach better than ever before.
