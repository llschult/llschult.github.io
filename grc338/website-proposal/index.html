Introduction

Sunny Side SLO

Sunny Side SLO is a Breakfast Cafe located in Avila beach. Customers order their food at a counter and find their own table to sit at. The products offered include main dishes such as sandwiches and waffles, drinks such as coffee and matcha, bakery items such as croissants and muffins, and sides such as bacon and eggs.

Target audience

Newer customers or tourists that aren’t familiar with the cafe who are looking to find more information such as hours of operation, location, and what types of products are offered.

Customers who have been to the cafe and are looking to either leave a review, ask a question, or give a suggestion, or they are looking at the menu again to see if they want to bring their family or friends back.
